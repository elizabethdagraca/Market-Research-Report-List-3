<p><p>Hot-melt adhesive market types include thermoplastic polyurethane, ethylene vinyl acetate, styrenic-butadiene copolymers, polyolefins, and polyamide. Styrenic-butadiene copolymers provide excellent heat resistance and flexibility. Polyolefins offer high strength and chemical resistance. Polyamide hot-melt adhesives are known for their high heat resistance and strong bonding capabilities. Each type caters to specific needs and applications in various industries.</p></p>
