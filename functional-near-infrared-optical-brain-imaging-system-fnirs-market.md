<p><p>Functional near infrared optical brain imaging system (fNIRS) market types include FT-NIR spectroscopy, dispersive NIR spectroscopy, and others such as AOTF and filter-based systems. FT-NIR spectroscopy utilizes Fourier-transform techniques for high-resolution measurements, while dispersive NIR spectroscopy disperses light into its spectral components for analysis. Other systems, like those using acousto-optic tunable filters (AOTF) or filters, offer alternative methods of wavelength selection and detection. These different types cater to various research and clinical applications in neuroscience and brain imaging.</p></p>
