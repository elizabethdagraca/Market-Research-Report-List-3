<p><p>Double fired furnaces are used in two main market types: atmosphere and vacuum. Atmosphere furnaces are designed to control the environment inside the furnace to prevent oxidation of the material being heated. This is particularly important for sensitive materials such as ceramics and metals. Vacuum furnaces, on the other hand, create a controlled environment with low pressure or absence of air to prevent contamination of the material. Both types of furnaces are essential in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and semiconductor for precise and high-quality heat treatment processes.</p></p>
