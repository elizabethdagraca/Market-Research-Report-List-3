<p><p>Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillators (TCXOs) have two main market types based on their shape: SMD Shape and PIN Shape. SMD Shape TCXOs are surface-mount devices that are compact and easy to integrate onto circuit boards, making them ideal for space-constrained applications. On the other hand, PIN Shape TCXOs have a pin-style housing that allows for easy installation and removal. Both types offer precise frequency stability over a wide temperature range, making them suitable for demanding temperature-sensitive applications such as telecommunications, aerospace, and military systems.</p></p>
