<p><p>The foreign exchange market consists of various types including currency swaps, outright forward and FX swaps, and FX options market. Currency swaps involve exchanging principal and interest payments in one currency for another. Outright forwards are agreements to exchange a specific amount of currency at a future date at an agreed upon rate. FX swaps involve the simultaneous purchase and sale of a currency for different value dates. FX options market gives the holder the right, but not the obligation, to buy or sell a currency at a specified exchange rate on or before a certain date.</p></p>
