I-CMTS integrates downstream and upstream components in a single chassis, offering a compact and cost-effective solution for operators. On the other hand, M-CMTS separates the downstream and upstream components into different modules, providing scalability and flexibility for operators to expand their network. Each market type caters to different operational needs and preferences within the cable industry.</p></p>
