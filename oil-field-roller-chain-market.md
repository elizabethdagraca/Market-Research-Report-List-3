<p><strong>The Oil Field Roller Chain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drive Chain</li><li>Conveyor Chain</li><li>Multi-Strand Chain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil field roller chains are categorized into three main types based on their specific applications: Drive chain, Conveyor chain, and Multi-strand chain. Drive chains are designed for transmitting power between rotating shafts, commonly used in oil field equipment such as pumps and compressors. Conveyor chains are used for moving bulk materials within the oil field operations, like in conveyor systems for transporting oil barrels or equipment. Multi-strand chains are used in heavy-duty applications where increased strength and durability are required, such as in high-torque machinery in oil fields.</p></p>
