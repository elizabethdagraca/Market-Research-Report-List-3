<p><p>Small road rollers can be classified into two main types: Single drum road rollers and double drum road rollers. Single drum road rollers have a large drum at the front of the vehicle, which is ideal for compacting soil, asphalt, and other materials on narrow roads and tight spaces. On the other hand, double drum road rollers have drums at the front and rear of the vehicle, allowing for efficient and uniform compaction of larger areas in a single pass. Both types are essential in road construction and maintenance projects.</p></p>
