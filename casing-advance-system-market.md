<p><strong>The Casing Advance System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Casing</li><li>Retrievable Casing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Permanent casing refers to a casing system that is installed and left in the wellbore permanently to provide structural support and casing protection. Retrievable casing is a system that allows for the casing to be retrieved or removed from the wellbore when needed for maintenance or intervention operations. Both types of casing systems are used in oil and gas wells to enhance well integrity, control fluid movement, and protect the well from external pressures and corrosive environments. Each type of casing advance system has its unique advantages and applications in the industry.</p></p>
