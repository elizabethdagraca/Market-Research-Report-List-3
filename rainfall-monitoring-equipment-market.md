<p><p>Rainfall monitoring equipment market offers various types of rain gauges to measure precipitation levels. Siphon rain gauge uses a siphoning mechanism, while tipping bucket rain gauge collects rain in a bucket that tips as it fills up. Ultrasonic rain gauge uses sound waves to calculate the amount of rainfall. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, with siphon and tipping bucket gauges being traditional mechanical options while ultrasonic gauges offer a more advanced and non-contact method of measuring rainfall.</p></p>
