<p><p>Electrical steel market is divided into two main types: grain-oriented and non-grain-oriented. Grain-oriented electrical steel is designed for use in transformers, where the orientation of the grains helps improve magnetic properties and reduce energy losses. Non-grain-oriented electrical steel, on the other hand, is used in motors, generators, and other electrical equipment where magnetic properties are important but grain orientation is not crucial. Both types of electrical steel play a vital role in the efficient operation of electrical equipment in various industries.</p></p>
