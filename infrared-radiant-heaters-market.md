<p><p>Infrared radiant heaters on the market are classified into two main types: high-intensity infrared and low-intensity infrared. High-intensity infrared heaters emit a more focused and intense heat, making them suitable for outdoor or industrial applications where targeted heating is needed. On the other hand, low-intensity infrared heaters provide a more gentle and widespread heat, making them ideal for indoor heating in residential or commercial spaces. Both types offer efficient and cost-effective heating solutions for different environments and purposes.</p></p>
