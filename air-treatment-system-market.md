HEPA filters are highly efficient in capturing particles, electrostatic precipitators use static electricity to trap particles, activated carbon filters remove odors and gases, UV filters kill bacteria and viruses, and ionic filters release ions to attract particles. Conventional filters are basic filters that trap larger particles. Each type of filter caters to different air purification needs.</p></p>
