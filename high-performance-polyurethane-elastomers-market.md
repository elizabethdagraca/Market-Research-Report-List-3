<p><p>High Performance Polyurethane Elastomers are classified into two main types: Thermoset PU Elastomers and Thermoplastic PU Elastomers. Thermoset PU Elastomers are cross-linked polymers that cannot be melted or reshaped once they are cured, making them ideal for applications requiring high durability and heat resistance. On the other hand, Thermoplastic PU Elastomers can be melted and reshaped multiple times, offering flexibility and ease of processing. Both types of PU Elastomers offer excellent mechanical properties and chemical resistance, making them popular choices in various industries.</p></p>
