<p><p>Automobile exhaust hoods come in two main types: single shell and double shell. Single shell hoods are constructed with a single layer of material and are generally more cost-effective, but offer less insulation. Double shell hoods have an additional layer of material for better insulation and noise reduction, making them more efficient but also more expensive. Both types play a crucial role in managing exhaust fumes and maintaining indoor air quality in automotive workshops and garages.</p></p>
