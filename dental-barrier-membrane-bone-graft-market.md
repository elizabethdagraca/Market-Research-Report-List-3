<p><ul><li>Absorbable Membrane</li><li>Non-absorbable Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental barrier membrane bone graft market includes absorbable and non-absorbable membranes. Absorbable membranes are made from materials that dissolve over time, eliminating the need for a second surgery to remove the membrane. They promote bone regeneration and are often used in guided bone regeneration procedures. Non-absorbable membranes are made from materials that do not dissolve and provide a barrier to protect the graft material and aid in bone regeneration. Both types of membranes play a crucial role in bone graft procedures in dentistry.</p></p>
