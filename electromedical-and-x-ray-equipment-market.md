<p><p>The electromedical and X-ray equipment market includes various types such as Digital Radiography (DR), Computed Radiography (CR), and Retrofit Radiography System. Digital Radiography (DR) uses digital detectors to produce X-ray images, while Computed Radiography (CR) uses imaging plates. Retrofit Radiography Systems are upgrades that convert existing analog X-ray systems into digital ones. These technologies offer quicker image processing, improved image quality, and increased efficiency in healthcare facilities. The market for these systems continues to grow as healthcare providers seek advanced diagnostic solutions.</p></p>
