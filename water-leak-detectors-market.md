<p><ul><li>Passive Leak Detector</li><li>Active Leak Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive leak detectors require no external power source and operate by absorbing water and triggering an alarm when detected. They are cost-effective and easy to install. Active leak detectors, on the other hand, are powered devices that actively monitor for leaks using sensors and alarms. They offer advanced features such as smartphone connectivity and automatic shut-off valves. Both types of detectors serve the same purpose of detecting water leaks but differ in their technology and capabilities.</p></p>
