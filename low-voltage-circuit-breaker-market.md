<p><p>The low-voltage circuit breaker market consists of three main types: Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB), Moulded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB), and Air Circuit Breaker (ACB). MCBs are commonly used for residential and light commercial applications, providing protection for short circuits and overloads. MCCBs are larger and more robust, suitable for higher current applications in industries and commercial buildings. ACBs are designed for heavy-duty industrial applications, providing higher breaking capacities and advanced protection features. Each type caters to specific voltage and current requirements in different settings.</p></p>
