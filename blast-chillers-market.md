<p><ul><li>Self-Contained Models</li><li>Roll-In Remote Condensing Unit Models</li><li>Roll-Thru Remote Condensing Unit Models</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blast chillers are divided into three main market types based on their condensing units: self-contained models, roll-in remote condensing unit models, and roll-thru remote condensing unit models. Self-contained models have the condensing unit built into the chiller itself, making them easier to install. Roll-in remote condensing unit models have an external condensing unit that can be located separately, while roll-thru remote condensing unit models allow for trays or racks to be wheeled in and out of the chiller easily.</p></p>
