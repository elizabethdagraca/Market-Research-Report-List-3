<p><p>Inline checkweighers are used to ensure the accurate measurement of the weight of a product during the production process. There are two main types of inline checkweighers - In-Motion Checkweighers and Intermittent Checkweighers. </p><p>In-Motion Checkweighers measure the weight of products as they move continuously along a conveyor belt, allowing for high-speed and efficient weight verification. </p><p>Intermittent Checkweighers, on the other hand, are used for products that cannot be weighed continuously, such as larger or irregularly shaped items. These checkweighers are designed to weigh products that are stopped intermittently during the production process.</p></p>
