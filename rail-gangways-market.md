<p><p>Rail gangways are an essential component of railway systems, facilitating safe passage between train cars. Single piece rail gangways are a continuous walkway connecting two train cars, providing easy access for passengers and crew. In contrast, two-piece rail gangways consist of separate sections that can be folded or extended to bridge the gap between cars. Both types offer convenience and safety for those moving between train carriages, with the choice between the two depending on specific operational requirements.</p></p>
