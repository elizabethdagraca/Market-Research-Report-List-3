<p><p>Ultrasound Non-destructive Testing (NDT) is a technique used to detect internal defects or flaws in materials without causing any damage to the object being tested. This method is commonly used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, manufacturing, and construction to ensure the quality and integrity of products.</p><p>The Ultrasound Non-destructive Testing (NDT) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.4% during the forecast period. <p><p>Ultrasound Non-destructive Testing (NDT) market includes various types of testing methodologies such as Ultrasonic Testing, Radiography Testing, Visual Inspection Testing, Magnetic Particle Testing, Electromagnetic Testing, Eddy-Current Testing, Liquid Penetrant Testing, and others.
