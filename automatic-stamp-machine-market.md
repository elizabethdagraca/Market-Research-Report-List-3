<p><p>Automatic stamp machines are categorized into two main types: semi-automatic and automatic. Semi-automatic stamp machines require some level of manual intervention, such as inputting data or selecting options before the stamp is printed. On the other hand, automatic stamp machines operate completely independently once the user initiates the process, requiring no further input. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences, with semi-automatic machines offering a balance between manual control and automation, while automatic machines provide a fully hands-free experience.</p></p>
