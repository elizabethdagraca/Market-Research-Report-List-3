<p><p>AMOLED panels are categorized into two main market types: flexible AMOLED panels and rigidity AMOLED panels. Flexible AMOLED panels are curved or bendable, allowing for innovative designs in smartphones and wearables. They offer enhanced durability and visibility due to their flexible nature. On the other hand, rigidity AMOLED panels are flat and inflexible, providing a more traditional display option for devices like TVs and monitors. Both types of panels offer high-quality imagery and energy efficiency, making them popular choices in the display market.</p></p>
