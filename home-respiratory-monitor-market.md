<p><p>The home respiratory monitor market includes various types of devices such as pulse oximeters, incentive spirometers, peak flow meters, and other monitors. Pulse oximeters are used to measure oxygen saturation levels in the blood, while incentive spirometers help improve lung function through deep breathing exercises. Peak flow meters are used to monitor the peak expiratory flow rate, which is important for managing conditions like asthma. The "other" category includes devices such as capnographs, gas analyzers, and respiratory rate monitors that help in monitoring respiratory health at home.</p></p>
