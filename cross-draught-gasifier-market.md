<p><p>Cross-draught gasifiers can be categorized into two main market types: Single Stage and Multiple Stages. Single Stage gasifiers have a simpler design and operate with a single combustion zone, making them more straightforward to manage. Multiple Stages gasifiers, on the other hand, have two or more combustion zones which allow for better control over the gasification process and can lead to higher efficiency and cleaner gas production. Both types of gasifiers have their own advantages and are used in various industries for converting biomass into syngas.</p></p>
