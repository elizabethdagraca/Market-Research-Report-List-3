<p><p>Dental fiberglass posts come in three main types in the market: tapered, parallel, and other variations. Tapered posts have a wider base that gradually narrows towards the tip, providing increased retention in tapered root canals. Parallel posts have a consistent diameter along their entire length, ideal for parallel-sided root canals. Other variations may include multiple tapers, serrations, or different materials. Dentists can choose the most suitable type of post based on the specific shape and size of the root canal to ensure a secure and long-lasting restoration.</p></p>
