<p><p>Medical aspirators are devices used to remove fluids, such as mucus or blood, from the body during medical procedures. There are three main types of medical aspirators on the market: electric, manual, and pneumatic. </p><p>Electric aspirators are powered by electricity and are suitable for continuous and high-volume suction. Manual aspirators rely on manual suction from the operator and are often used in emergency situations. Pneumatic aspirators utilize air pressure to create suction and are commonly used in surgical settings for precise and controlled aspiration.</p></p>
