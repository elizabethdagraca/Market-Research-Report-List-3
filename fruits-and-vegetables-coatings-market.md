<p><p>Fruit and vegetable coatings are used to prolong the shelf life and maintain the freshness of produce. Moisture-proof coatings prevent moisture loss, while antigas coatings help slow down the ripening process by inhibiting the production of ethylene gas. Ethylene production inhibits coatings are specifically designed to regulate the levels of ethylene gas to prevent premature ripening and spoilage. These different types of coatings offer a variety of solutions for extending the quality and shelf life of fruits and vegetables in the market.</p></p>
