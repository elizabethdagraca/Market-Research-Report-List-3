<p><strong>The Hot Runner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Gate Hot Runner</li><li>Valve Gate Hot Runner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot runner systems are used in injection molding to keep the plastic material in a molten state within the runner system, allowing for faster and more efficient production. Open gate hot runner systems have a simple design with no physical gate at the mold cavity entrance, while valve gate hot runner systems have a valve mechanism at the gate to precisely control the flow of plastic material. Both types offer advantages in terms of reduced waste, improved part quality, and enhanced production speed.</p></p>
