<p><p>Organ Preservation Techniques market includes three main types: Static Cold Storage, Organ Preservation Fluids, and Machine Perfusion Techniques. Static Cold Storage involves placing organs in a cold solution to slow down metabolic processes and preserve them for transplantation. Organ Preservation Fluids are solutions that provide nutrients and oxygen to organs during storage. Machine Perfusion Techniques use machines to pump oxygenated blood or preservation solutions through organs, allowing for better preservation and assessment of organ viability before transplantation.</p></p>
