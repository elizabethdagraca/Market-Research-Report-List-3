<p><p>Starch processing enzymes market includes various types such as alpha-amylase, beta-amylase, and amyloglucosidase. Alpha-amylase enzymes are used to break down starch molecules into shorter chains, while beta-amylase enzymes specifically target the ends of the starch chains. Amyloglucosidase enzymes further break down the shorter starch chains into glucose molecules. Other types of enzymes in the market may include proteases, cellulases, and hemicellulases, which also play important roles in starch processing. These enzymes are essential in various industries such as food, beverage, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
