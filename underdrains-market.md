<p><p>Underdrains are drainage systems used in various applications such as flat surfaces, filter covers, cone-shaped installations, and horizontal culverts. Flat underdrains are commonly used in agricultural fields and sports fields to prevent waterlogging. Filter cover underdrains are used in stormwater management systems to filter out debris and pollutants. Cone-shaped underdrains are typically used in residential and commercial construction projects to remove excess water from the ground. Horizontal culvert underdrains are used in roadways and highways to manage runoff and prevent erosion.</p></p>
