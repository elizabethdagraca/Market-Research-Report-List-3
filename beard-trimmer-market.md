<p><p>Beard trimmers come in two main types: reciprocating and rotary. Reciprocating trimmers have blades that move back and forth in a linear motion, providing a precise and clean shave. They are ideal for creating sharp lines and defining edges. On the other hand, rotary trimmers have blades that spin in a circular motion, making them efficient at tackling longer or thicker hair. Both types cater to different styling needs and preferences, offering a diverse range of options for grooming and maintaining facial hair.</p></p>
