<p><p>Commercial Organic Bread Flour can be categorized into two main types based on the milling process: Machine Mill Flour and Stone Mill Flour. Machine Mill Flour is produced using automated machinery, which results in a more fine and uniform texture. On the other hand, Stone Mill Flour is ground using traditional stone mills, which preserves more of the wheat's natural oils and nutrients, giving it a more rustic and flavorful quality. Both types of flour cater to different consumer preferences and are widely used in the baking industry.</p></p>
