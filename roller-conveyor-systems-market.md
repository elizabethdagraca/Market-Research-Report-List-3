<p><ul><li>Chain-Driven Roller Conveyor</li><li>Powered Roller Conveyor</li><li>Gravity Roller Conveyor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Roller conveyor systems are commonly classified into three main types: Chain-Driven Roller Conveyor, Powered Roller Conveyor, and Gravity Roller Conveyor. Chain-Driven Roller Conveyors use chains to drive the rollers and are suitable for heavy-duty applications. Powered Roller Conveyors utilize motors to move products along the conveyor line smoothly. Gravity Roller Conveyors rely on the force of gravity to move products along the conveyor path without the need for external power sources. Each type caters to specific needs and applications within the material handling industry.</p></p>
