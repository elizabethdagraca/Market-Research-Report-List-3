<p><ul><li>Active LCD Monitors</li><li>Passive LCD Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Professional Broadcast LCD Monitor market can be categorized into two types: Active LCD Monitors and Passive LCD Monitors. Active LCD Monitors utilize a backlight to produce images with high brightness and vibrant colors, making them ideal for applications where image quality is crucial. Passive LCD Monitors, on the other hand, do not have a backlight and rely on external lighting sources, resulting in lower energy consumption and reduced eye strain. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the professional broadcast industry.</p></p>
