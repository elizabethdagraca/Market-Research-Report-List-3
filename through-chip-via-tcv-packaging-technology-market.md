<p><p>Through-Chip-Via (TCV) Packaging Technology involves creating vertical interconnects within a chip to enhance performance and reduce power consumption. There are three main types of TCV packaging: Via First TCV, Via Middle TCV, and Via Last TCV. Via First TCV places vias before any other layers are added, Via Middle TCV integrates vias in the middle of the packaging process, and Via Last TCV adds vias as the final step. Each type offers unique benefits and trade-offs depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
