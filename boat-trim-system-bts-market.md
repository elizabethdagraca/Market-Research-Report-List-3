<p><ul><li>Trim Tabs</li><li>Interceptors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Boat Trim System (BTS) market consists of two main types: Trim Tabs and Interceptors. Trim Tabs are adjustable devices mounted on the back of a boat's hull to control the trim angle, improve stability, and enhance overall performance. Interceptors are adjustable plates mounted on the transom that can be raised or lowered to adjust the boat's running attitude and reduce drag. Both trim tab and interceptor systems play a crucial role in improving a boat's handling and efficiency on the water.</p></p>
