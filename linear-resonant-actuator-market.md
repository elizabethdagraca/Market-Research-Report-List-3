<p><p>Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAs) are commonly found in two main types - Rectangular LRAs and Coin Type LRAs. Rectangular LRAs are larger and typically used in applications where a higher force output is required. Coin Type LRAs are smaller and more compact, making them suitable for smaller devices such as smartphones and wearables. Both types operate by vibrating at their resonant frequency to produce haptic feedback in devices, providing users with tactile sensations like vibrations and clicks.</p></p>
