<p><p>Hydraulic metering pumps are categorized into different types based on their operating mechanisms. Diaphragm hydraulic metering pumps use a flexible diaphragm to displace fluid, providing accurate and consistent flow rates. Piston hydraulic metering pumps use a reciprocating piston to achieve precise metering of fluids. Other types of hydraulic metering pumps may include gear pumps, rotary pumps, or peristaltic pumps. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in industries such as chemical processing, water treatment, and oil & gas.</p></p>
