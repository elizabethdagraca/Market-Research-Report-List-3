<p><p>Bonded carbide market types such as R5, GT35, TM52, TM60, and GW1 are differentiated based on their unique compositions and properties. R5 carbides are known for their high wear resistance, suitable for cutting tools. GT35 carbides offer improved impact resistance and toughness, making them ideal for machining applications. TM52 carbides provide excellent heat resistance, perfect for high-temperature operations. TM60 carbides are favored for their superior corrosion resistance. Lastly, GW1 carbides are known for their exceptional hardness and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty cutting tasks.</p></p>
