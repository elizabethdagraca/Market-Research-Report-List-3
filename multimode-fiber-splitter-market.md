<p><p>Multimode fiber splitters come in various types depending on the number of input and output ports they have. The most common types include 1×2, 1×4, 1×8, and 2×2 splitters. A 1×2 splitter has one input and two output ports, a 1×4 splitter has one input and four output ports, and a 1×8 splitter has one input and eight output ports. A 2×2 splitter has two input and two output ports. Other types of multimode fiber splitters may have different configurations based on specific requirements.</p></p>
