<p><p>Blood test equipment market includes various types of devices used for testing different components of blood. Haemoglobin meters measure the amount of haemoglobin in the blood, blood cell counters determine the number and types of blood cells present, and Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate (ESR) analyzers measure the rate at which red blood cells settle in a sample of blood. Other types of blood test equipment may include coagulation analyzers, electrolyte analyzers, and glucose meters among others, which help in diagnosing various health conditions.</p></p>
