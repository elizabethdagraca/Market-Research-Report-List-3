<p><p>Helicobacter Pylori Non-Invasive Testing Market comprises of three main types: Serology Test, Stool Antigen Test, and Urea Breath Test. Serology Test detects antibodies in the blood, indicating the presence of H. pylori. Stool Antigen Test detects H. pylori antigens in stool samples. Urea Breath Test involves ingesting a urea solution labeled with a radioactive or non-radioactive carbon atom, followed by breath analysis to detect the presence of H. pylori. These tests are simple, non-invasive ways to diagnose H. pylori infection.</p></p>
