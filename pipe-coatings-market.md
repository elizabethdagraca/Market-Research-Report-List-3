<p><p>Pipe coatings are commonly categorized as liquid and powder coatings in the market. Liquid coatings are applied as a liquid solution, which forms a protective layer once dried. They are easy to apply and provide good adhesion to the pipe surface. On the other hand, powder coatings are applied as dry powder and then heated to form a protective layer. They offer better corrosion resistance and durability compared to liquid coatings. Both liquid and powder coatings are widely utilized in the pipe industry for corrosion protection and extending the lifespan of pipes.</p></p>
