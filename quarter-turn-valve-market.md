<p><p>Quarter-turn valves are categorized into different types based on their design and operation. Ball type valves have a spherical closure element that rotates 90 degrees to control flow. Globe type valves have a plug with a flat or conical bottom that moves up and down to regulate flow. Plug type valves have a cylindrical or tapered plug that rotates to open or close the valve. Other types include butterfly valves, diaphragm valves, and pinch valves, each with unique mechanisms for flow control.</p></p>
