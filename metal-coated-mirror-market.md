<p><ul><li>First Surface Mirror</li><li>Second Surface Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal-coated mirrors are categorized into two types based on the surface where the metal coating is applied. First surface mirrors have the metal coating on the front surface, allowing for minimal light distortion and high reflectivity. Second surface mirrors have the metal coating on the back surface, providing protection against scratches and corrosion. Both types of mirrors are used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and optics for applications requiring high precision and quality reflective surfaces.</p></p>
