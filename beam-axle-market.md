<p><strong>The Beam Axle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dead Axle</li><li>Live Axle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A dead axle is a type of beam axle where the wheels are mounted on a fixed beam that does not rotate. This setup is commonly seen in trailers and some agricultural equipment. On the other hand, a live axle refers to a beam axle where the wheels are mounted on a beam that rotates along with the differential. Live axles are commonly used in vehicles like trucks and SUVs for their ability to provide better traction and stability on rough terrain.</p></p>
