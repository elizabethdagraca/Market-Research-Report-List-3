<p><p>Respiratory Protection Equipment (RPE) market consists of various types including Negative Pressure Air-Purifying Respirators (APR), Positive Pressure Powered Air-Purifying Respirators (PAPR), and Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA). Negative Pressure APRs rely on the wearer's respiratory effort, while Positive Pressure PAPRs use a powered fan to assist with breathing. SCBAs are self-contained units with a supply of compressed air for breathing. Each type offers different levels of protection and comfort, catering to various industries and working environments.</p></p>
