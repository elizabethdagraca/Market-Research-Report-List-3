<p><p>The Ceramic Metering Pump market includes various types such as Hydraulic Diaphragm Type, Electromagnetic Diaphragm Type, and Mechanical Plunger Type. Hydraulic Diaphragm Type pumps are operated using hydraulic forces to displace the diaphragm and control the flow rate. Electromagnetic Diaphragm Type pumps use magnetic forces to move the diaphragm and regulate the flow. Mechanical Plunger Type pumps use mechanical plungers to adjust the flow rate. Each type offers unique features and benefits, catering to different industry requirements.</p></p>
