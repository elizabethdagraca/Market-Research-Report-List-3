<p><p>The continuous dust monitor market can be categorized into two main types: portable and stationary. Portable dust monitors are designed for on-the-go measurements and are typically used for personal exposure monitoring or area sampling. Stationary dust monitors, on the other hand, are permanently installed in specific locations such as industrial sites or factories to continuously monitor dust levels over an extended period of time. Both types of monitors play a crucial role in ensuring air quality regulations are met and in protecting workers' health.</p></p>
