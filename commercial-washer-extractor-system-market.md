<p><p>Commercial washer extractor systems are commonly categorized into two main types: open pocket and divided-cylinder. Open pocket systems have a single large drum where laundry is loaded and washed all together. Divided-cylinder systems, on the other hand, feature separate compartments within the drum to wash different types of fabrics or separate loads simultaneously. Both types offer efficient washing and extraction capabilities for commercial use, but the choice between them depends on specific needs and requirements of the user.</p></p>
