On the other hand, Class 2 Ceramic Capacitors offer higher capacitance values and are suitable for decoupling and filtering applications in RF and microwave circuits. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring proper signal transmission and efficiency in electronic devices operating at high frequencies.</p></p>
