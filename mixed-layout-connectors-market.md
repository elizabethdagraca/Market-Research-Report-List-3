<p><p>Mixed layout connectors are divided into two main types - Pin Contact and Socket Contact. Pin Contact connectors have a series of metal pins that connect to corresponding sockets in the mating connector. On the other hand, Socket Contact connectors have sockets that connect to pins in the mating connector. Both types are commonly used in various industries for their ability to provide secure and reliable connections between different types of devices and systems.</p></p>
