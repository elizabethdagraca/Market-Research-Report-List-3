<p><p>The Robots Harmonic Drive market can be categorized into three main types: Cup Style, Hat Style, and Pancake Style. Cup Style harmonic drives have a circular shape with an internal cup that holds the gear. Hat Style drives have a flat shape with a gear mounted on top like a hat. Pancake Style drives are thin and flat, resembling a pancake. Each type offers unique characteristics and advantages based on the specific requirements of the application, making them suitable for a variety of robotic systems.</p></p>
