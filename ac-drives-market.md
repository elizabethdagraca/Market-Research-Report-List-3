<p><p>AC drives market is divided into two main types based on voltage requirements: Low Voltage and Medium Voltage. Low voltage AC drives are typically used in applications that require power up to 690V, such as pumps, fans, and conveyors. They are more common in industries like manufacturing, mining, and oil and gas. On the other hand, medium voltage AC drives are used in applications requiring power above 690V, such as large motors and compressors. They are commonly found in industries like utilities, power generation, and heavy manufacturing.</p></p>
