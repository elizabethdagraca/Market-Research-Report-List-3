<p><strong>The Ambulatory Cardiac Monitoring Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Holter Monitor</li><li>Event Monitors</li><li>Mobile Cardiac Telemetry</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ambulatory cardiac monitoring equipment includes various types such as Holter monitors, event monitors, mobile cardiac telemetry devices, and others. Holter monitors are portable devices that continuously record a patient's heart activity for 24-48 hours. Event monitors are used on an as-needed basis to record heart rhythm abnormalities. Mobile cardiac telemetry devices are more advanced, providing real-time monitoring and transmission of data to healthcare providers. Other types of ambulatory cardiac monitoring equipment may include patch monitors or implantable loop recorders.</p></p>
