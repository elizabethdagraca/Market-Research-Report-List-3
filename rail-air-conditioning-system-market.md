<p><strong>The Rail Air-Conditioning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Cycle Air Conditioning Systems</li><li>Vapor Cycle Air Conditioning Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air cycle air conditioning systems utilize the expansion of compressed air to cool the cabin of a rail vehicle. This system does not require any refrigerant or compressor, making it environmentally friendly. On the other hand, vapor cycle air conditioning systems use a refrigerant to absorb and release heat, providing efficient cooling. Both types of systems offer cooling solutions for rail vehicles, with air cycle systems being more eco-friendly and vapor cycle systems being more energy-efficient.</p></p>
