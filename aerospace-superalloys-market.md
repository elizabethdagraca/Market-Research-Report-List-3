<p><ul><li>Nickel Base Superalloy</li><li>Nickel Iron Base Superalloy</li><li>Cobalt Base Superalloy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nickel base superalloys are a type of aerospace superalloy that primarily consists of nickel with added elements such as chromium, cobalt, and iron. These alloys are known for their excellent high-temperature strength, corrosion resistance, and oxidation resistance, making them ideal for use in aircraft engines and gas turbines. Nickel iron base superalloys also contain nickel as the primary element, but with added iron for improved mechanical properties. Cobalt base superalloys, on the other hand, are composed mainly of cobalt with elements like chromium, tungsten, and nickel for high-temperature applications.</p></p>
