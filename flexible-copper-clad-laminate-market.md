<p><p>Flexible copper-clad laminate market types consist of tape casting, sputtering, electroplating, and laminating. Tape casting involves the use of a flexible polymer substrate coated with copper particles. Sputtering is a process where copper is deposited on a flexible substrate through high-energy particle bombardment. Electroplating involves the electrochemical deposition of copper onto a flexible substrate. Laminating is the process of bonding copper layers onto a flexible substrate using heat and pressure. Each method offers unique advantages and applications within the flexible copper-clad laminate industry.</p></p>
