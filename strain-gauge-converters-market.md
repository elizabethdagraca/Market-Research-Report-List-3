<p><p>Strain gauge converters for current output and voltage output are two types of devices used to convert the small changes in resistance from strain gauges into measurable electrical signals. Current output converters provide a proportional current signal as the output, while voltage output converters provide a proportional voltage signal. Both types of converters are widely used in industries where precise measurements of strain and stress are required, such as in structural monitoring, automotive testing, and material testing applications.</p></p>
