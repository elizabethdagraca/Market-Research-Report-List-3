<p><ul><li>IOLs</li><li>OVDs</li><li>Phacoemulsification Devices</li><li>Femtosecond Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cataract Devices Market consists of various types of devices used in the treatment of cataracts. Intraocular lenses (IOLs) are implanted during cataract surgery to replace the eye's natural lens. Ophthalmic viscosurgical devices (OVDs) are used to protect and maintain the shape of the eye during surgery. Phacoemulsification devices are used to break up and remove the cloudy lens. Femtosecond lasers are advanced tools that assist in creating precise incisions during cataract surgery. These devices collectively play a crucial role in improving vision and quality of life for cataract patients.</p></p>
