<p><p>Tractor transmission systems can be categorized into mechanical and hydraulic types. Mechanical transmission systems use gears, shafts, and other mechanical components to transfer power from the engine to the wheels. They are known for their durability and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, hydraulic transmission systems use fluid power to transfer energy, offering smoother operation and flexibility in terms of speed and torque control. Both types have their own advantages and cater to different needs in the tractor market.</p></p>
