<p><ul><li>Slow freezing</li><li>Vitrification</li><li>Ultra-rapid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryonics technology market types include slow freezing, vitrification, and ultra-rapid methods. Slow freezing involves gradually reducing the temperature of the body over hours or days to minimize ice crystal formation. Vitrification uses cryoprotectants to prevent ice formation entirely, preserving the body's structure. Ultra-rapid methods are designed to quickly lower the body's temperature to prevent damage. Each type of cryonics technology has its own advantages and limitations, appealing to different customers based on their preferences and beliefs.</p></p>
