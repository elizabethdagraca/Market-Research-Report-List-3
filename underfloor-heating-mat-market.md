<p><p>Underfloor Heating Mat Market Types include Electric and Water-based systems. Electric systems use heating cables embedded in a mat to generate heat, while Water-based systems use heated water flowing through pipes beneath the floor. Electric systems are easier and more cost-effective to install, making them popular for renovations. Water-based systems are known for their efficiency and even heat distribution, making them commonly used in new constructions. Both types offer a comfortable and energy-efficient way to heat a room without the need for radiators or ductwork.</p></p>
