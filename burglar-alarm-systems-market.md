<p><p>Burglar alarm systems are categorized into two main types: wireless alarm systems and wired alarm systems. Wireless alarm systems use radio frequency technology to communicate between sensors and the control panel, providing flexibility in installation and easier integration with smart home devices. On the other hand, wired alarm systems require physical wiring to connect sensors to the control panel, offering a more reliable connection but requiring professional installation. Both types of alarm systems offer different features and benefits to cater to the diverse needs of consumers.</p></p>
