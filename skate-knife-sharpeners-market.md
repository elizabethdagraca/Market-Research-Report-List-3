<p><ul><li>Automatic</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Skate knife sharpeners come in two main types: automatic and manual. Automatic sharpeners are electric-powered machines that use rotating abrasive discs to quickly and efficiently sharpen skate blades. On the other hand, manual sharpeners require human effort to sharpen the blades using handheld tools like sharpening stones or honing rods. Manual sharpeners are often preferred by those who enjoy the hands-on experience of sharpening their skates, while automatic sharpeners are popular among those looking for a quick and consistent sharpening process.</p></p>
