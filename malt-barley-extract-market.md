Light extracts are pale in color and have a mild flavor, making them popular in lighter beer styles. Amber extracts have a richer color and a more pronounced malt flavor, ideal for amber ales and red ales. Dark extracts are the darkest in color and have a strong, roasted malt flavor, commonly used in stouts and porters. Each type of extract caters to different beer styles and flavor profiles.</p></p>
