<p><p>Waveguide components and assemblies market types include adapters, couplers, isolators and circulators, duplexers, pressure windows, and others. Adapters are used to connect waveguides with different sizes or shapes. Couplers split or combine microwave power. Isolators and circulators ensure signal directionality and prevent back reflections. Duplexers allow for simultaneous transmission and reception. Pressure windows maintain waveguide integrity under high-pressure conditions. Other components may include terminations, transitions, filters, and power dividers. These components play essential roles in various RF and microwave applications.</p></p>
