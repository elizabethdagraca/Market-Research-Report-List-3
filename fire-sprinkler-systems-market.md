<p><ul><li>Wet Pipe Fire Sprinkler Systems</li><li>Dry Pipe Fire Sprinkler Systems</li><li>Deluge Fire Sprinkler Systems</li><li>Pre-Action Fire Sprinkler Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire Sprinkler Systems market includes Wet Pipe, Dry Pipe, Deluge, and Pre-Action systems. Wet Pipe systems are the most common, with water constantly in the pipes ready to be released when a fire is detected. Dry Pipe systems are filled with air until a fire triggers the valve to release water. Deluge systems release water over a wide area when a fire is detected. Pre-Action systems combine elements of both wet and dry pipe systems, requiring two triggers before water is released.</p></p>
