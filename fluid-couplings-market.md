<p><ul><li>Common Type</li><li>Torque Limiting Type</li><li>Speed Regulation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluid couplings are widely used in various industries for power transmission and torque amplification. The common type of fluid coupling provides smooth and reliable torque transfer between input and output shafts. Torque limiting fluid couplings are designed to protect equipment from overload conditions by limiting the torque transmitted. Speed regulation fluid couplings are used to maintain a constant speed between input and output shafts, even under varying load conditions. Each type of fluid coupling offers specific benefits in terms of power transmission and equipment protection, catering to the diverse needs of industries.</p></p>
