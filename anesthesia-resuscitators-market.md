There are various types of anesthesia resuscitators available in the market including face masks, combitubes, Magill forceps, and tracheal tubes. Face masks are used to cover the nose and mouth to deliver anesthesia, combitubes are alternative airway devices, Magill forceps are used to remove foreign bodies from the airway, and tracheal tubes are inserted into the windpipe to maintain a clear airway during anesthesia. Each type serves a different purpose in ensuring proper ventilation and oxygenation for patients.</p></p>
