<p><p>Synthetic silicon fertilizers come in two main types: liquid and solid. Liquid silicon fertilizers are easy to apply, as they can be sprayed directly onto plants, while solid silicon fertilizers are typically granular or powdered forms that can be spread onto the soil. Both types of silicon fertilizers provide plants with essential nutrients for healthy growth and improved resistance to pests and diseases.</p></p>
