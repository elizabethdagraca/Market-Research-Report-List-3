<p><p>Glass laser scribing systems are commonly categorized into two types based on the wavelength of the laser used: Infrared Laser and UV Laser. Infrared lasers are suitable for scribing glass materials due to their longer wavelengths that are absorbed well by the material, allowing for efficient processing. On the other hand, UV lasers are ideal for precise and intricate scribing on glass surfaces, thanks to their shorter wavelengths and high energy output. Both types offer unique advantages for different applications in the glass industry.</p></p>
