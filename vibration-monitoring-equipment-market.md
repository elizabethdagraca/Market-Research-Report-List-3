<p><p>Vibration monitoring equipment can be classified into two main types: offline and online. Offline vibration monitoring equipment is typically used for periodic data collection and analysis, where the sensor is attached to the machine and data is manually downloaded for analysis. On the other hand, online vibration monitoring equipment continuously monitors machine vibrations in real-time, providing instant alerts for any abnormal behavior. Both types are crucial for preventive maintenance to detect potential issues and avoid costly downtime.</p></p>
