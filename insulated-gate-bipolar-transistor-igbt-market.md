<p><p>The Insulated-Gate Bipolar Transistor (IGBT) market can be categorized into two main types: Discrete IGBT and Modular IGBT. Discrete IGBTs are standalone components that are used in various applications such as power supplies, motor drives, and inverters. On the other hand, Modular IGBTs are integrated modules that combine various components, including IGBTs, diodes, and gate drivers, into a single package. Modular IGBTs are commonly used in high-power applications where compactness and efficiency are crucial.</p></p>
