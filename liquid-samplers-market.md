<p><ul><li>Single Point</li><li>Cross Section Sampling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid samplers market can be categorized into two main types: single point sampling and cross section sampling. Single point samplers are used to collect samples at a specific point within a liquid stream, providing a snapshot of the composition at that exact location. On the other hand, cross section samplers collect samples across the entire cross section of the liquid stream, giving a more comprehensive representation of the overall composition. Both types of samplers are important tools for quality control and monitoring in various industries.</p></p>
