AC motors, also known as alternating current motors, are commonly used in applications where constant speed is required, such as household appliances. On the other hand, DC motors, or direct current motors, are preferred in applications where variable speed control is needed, like in battery-operated devices. Each type of motor has its own advantages and disadvantages, making them suitable for different types of applications in the electric micro-motors market.</p></p>
