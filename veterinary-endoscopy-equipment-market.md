<p><p>Veterinary endoscopy equipment market can be divided into four main types: flexible endoscopy, rigid endoscopy, capsule endoscopy, and others. Flexible endoscopy involves the use of a flexible tube with a camera and light source to examine internal organs. Rigid endoscopy uses a rigid tube for better control during procedures. Capsule endoscopy involves swallowing a small camera pill to take images as it passes through the digestive system. Other types of endoscopy equipment may include video endoscopy systems and fiberoptic endoscopes.</p></p>
