<p><ul><li>Portable Dehumidifier</li><li>Whole-home Dehumidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Swimming pool dehumidifiers come in two main types: portable dehumidifiers and whole-home dehumidifiers. Portable dehumidifiers are smaller units that can be easily moved around and are typically used for smaller residential pools or for spot dehumidification. Whole-home dehumidifiers, on the other hand, are larger units designed to dehumidify the entire indoor space where the swimming pool is located, making them ideal for larger pools or commercial facilities. Both types of dehumidifiers help maintain proper humidity levels in indoor pool areas to prevent mold, mildew, and corrosion.</p></p>
