<p><p>Medical protective wear market can be categorized into three types: surgical wear, daily work wear, and special protective wear. Surgical wear includes gowns, masks, and gloves worn by healthcare professionals during surgical procedures to maintain sterile conditions. Daily work wear consists of scrubs, lab coats, and protective eyewear worn by healthcare workers in their daily routine. Special protective wear includes suits, respirators, and goggles used in high-risk environments such as handling hazardous materials or infectious diseases. Each type serves a specific purpose in ensuring safety and hygiene in medical settings.</p></p>
