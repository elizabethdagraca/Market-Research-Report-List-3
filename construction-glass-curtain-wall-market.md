<p><p>The construction glass curtain wall market is segmented into two main types: unitized type and stick built type. Unitized type involves pre-assembled panels that are lifted and attached to the building structure, offering quicker installation and better insulation. Stick built type involves individual glass panels that are installed on-site piece by piece, allowing for more customization but taking longer to complete. Both types offer different advantages and are chosen based on factors such as project timeline, budget, and architectural design requirements.</p></p>
