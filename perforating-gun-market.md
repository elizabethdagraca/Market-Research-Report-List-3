<p><strong>The Perforating Gun Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>WCP Type</li><li>TCP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Perforating Gun market includes two main types: Wireline Conveyed Perforating (WCP) and Tubing Conveyed Perforating (TCP). WCP involves the use of a wireline to convey the perforating gun downhole to create holes in the well casing. TCP, on the other hand, uses tubing to convey the gun and is often used in deviated wells or wells with complex trajectories. Both types play a crucial role in the oil and gas industry by enabling the creation of perforations to increase production from reservoirs.</p></p>
