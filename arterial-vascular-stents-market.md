<p><strong>The Arterial Vascular Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self Expansion</li><li>Balloon Dilatation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Arterial vascular stents are devices used to treat blockages in the blood vessels. There are two main types of arterial vascular stents: self-expanding stents and balloon dilatation stents. </p><p>Self-expanding stents are made of a metal mesh that expands on its own once it is inserted into the blocked vessel, providing support and keeping the vessel open. </p><p>On the other hand, balloon dilatation stents are deployed using a balloon that is inflated to compress the blockage and widen the vessel.</p><p>Both types of stents are used to improve blood flow and prevent complications related to narrowed or blocked arteries.</p></p>
