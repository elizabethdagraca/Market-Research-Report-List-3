<p><p>Intelligent Dosimetry Equipment includes Thermoluminescent Dosimetry (TLD) and Optically Stimulated Luminescence (OSL) tools that measure radiation exposure in various environments such as medical facilities, research laboratories, and industrial settings. TLD devices measure ionizing radiation by detecting the intensity of light emitted when a crystal is heated, while OSL equipment measures radiation exposure by stimulating trapped electrons to emit light. Both types of dosimetry equipment are essential for monitoring and maintaining safe radiation levels for workers and the general public.</p></p>
