<p><p>Crankshaft oil market is divided into two main types: liquid and semi-solid. Liquid crankshaft oils are commonly used in engines due to their ability to easily flow and lubricate various engine components. They are ideal for use in vehicles that require frequent cold starts and high operating temperatures. On the other hand, semi-solid crankshaft oils are designed for heavy-duty applications where extreme pressure and high temperatures are common. They provide long-lasting lubrication and protection to the engine under harsh operating conditions.</p></p>
