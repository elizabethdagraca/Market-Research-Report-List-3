Water scrubbers use water to remove impurities from biogas, while chemical scrubbers use chemicals to purify it. PSA systems separate gases based on their molecular properties, and membrane systems use membranes to separate gases. Each type of equipment offers different advantages and applications for upgrading biogas to meet quality specifications for use as a renewable energy source.</p></p>
