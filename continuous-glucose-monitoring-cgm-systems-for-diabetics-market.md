<p><p>Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) systems for diabetics are devices that help individuals measure and monitor their blood glucose levels constantly. These systems come in two main types: wearable and implantable. Wearable CGM systems are external devices that are attached to the skin, providing real-time glucose readings and alerts. Implantable CGM systems are inserted under the skin and continuously monitor glucose levels, eliminating the need for constant sensor replacement. Both types aim to help diabetics manage their blood sugar levels more effectively.</p></p>
