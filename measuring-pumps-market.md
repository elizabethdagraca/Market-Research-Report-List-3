<p><p>Measuring pumps are categorized into two main types: fixed displacement and variable displacement. Fixed displacement measuring pumps deliver a consistent amount of fluid per revolution, making them ideal for applications requiring precise and constant flow rates. On the other hand, variable displacement measuring pumps allow users to adjust the flow rate according to changing process requirements. Both types offer reliable and accurate fluid delivery, catering to a wide range of industries such as chemical processing, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
