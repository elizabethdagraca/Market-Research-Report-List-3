<p><p>Closed System Drug Transfer Devices are designed to minimize the risk of exposure to hazardous drugs during their handling and administration. Two main types of Closed System Drug Transfer Devices are Membrane-to-Membrane Systems and Needleless Systems. Membrane-to-Membrane Systems utilize a membrane to transfer drugs between containers without exposing healthcare workers to the drug. Needleless Systems use a needleless connector to transfer drugs, reducing the risk of needlestick injuries and contamination. Both types of devices offer enhanced safety and protection for healthcare personnel.</p></p>
