<p><ul><li>UV curing</li><li>Visible light-curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dual-curing adhesives encompass two main types: UV curing and visible light-curing. UV curing adhesives utilize ultraviolet light to initiate the curing process, ideal for applications that require rapid curing and strong bond strength. Visible light-curing adhesives, on the other hand, use visible light to cure, offering a more controllable and versatile curing process. Both types of adhesives are commonly used in industries such as electronics, medical devices, and automotive, where fast and reliable bonding is essential.</p></p>
