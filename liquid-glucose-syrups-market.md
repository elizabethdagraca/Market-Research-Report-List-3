<p><strong>The Liquid Glucose Syrups Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Conversion 20-35 DE</li><li>Intermediate Conversion 35-55 DE</li><li>High Conversion 55-70 DE</li><li>Very High Conversion 70-98 DE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid glucose syrups are classified based on their dextrose equivalent (DE) levels, which determines their sweetness and functional properties. Low conversion syrups (20-35 DE) are less sweet and commonly used in beverages and dairy products. Intermediate conversion syrups (35-55 DE) have moderate sweetness and are used in confectionery and bakery products. High conversion syrups (55-70 DE) are very sweet and commonly used in ice creams and jams. Very high conversion syrups (70-98 DE) are extremely sweet and serve as a binding agent in food products.</p></p>
