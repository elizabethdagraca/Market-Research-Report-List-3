<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vertical in-place inclinometers are used to measure the inclination or tilt of a structure or embankment in the vertical direction. These inclinometers are typically installed vertically within the structure and provide real-time data on any movement or change in inclination.</p><p>Level in-place inclinometers, on the other hand, are used to measure the levelness or flatness of a surface or structure. They are installed horizontally and provide accurate readings on any deviations from a perfectly level position. Both types of in-place inclinometers are used in various construction, geotechnical, and monitoring applications.</p></p>
