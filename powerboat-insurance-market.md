<p><strong>The Powerboat Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Actual Cash Value</li><li>Agreed Amount Value</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powerboat insurance is available in two main market types: Actual Cash Value (ACV) and Agreed Amount Value. ACV covers the current market value of the powerboat at the time of the claim, taking depreciation into account. Agreed Amount Value, on the other hand, allows the policyholder and the insurer to agree on a set value for the powerboat upfront, which will be paid out in the event of a total loss. Each market type offers different levels of coverage and premiums, catering to the specific needs of powerboat owners.</p></p>
