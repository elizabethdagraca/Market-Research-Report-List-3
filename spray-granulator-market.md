<p><p>A spray granulator is a type of granulation equipment used to create granules by spraying a liquid binder onto a powder material. This process helps in controlling the size, density, and composition of the granules, making them suitable for various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemicals.</p><p>The spray granulator market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9% during the forecast period. The key factors driving this growth include the increasing demand for granulated products in industries such as pharmaceuticals and food processing, as well as the rising investments in research and development activities to enhance the efficiency of granulation processes.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the spray granulator market is the adoption of advanced technologies such as fluidized bed granulation and melt granulation, which offer improved control over the granule properties and reduce processing times. Additionally, the growing focus on sustainability and environmental concerns is driving the development of eco-friendly granulation processes using organic binders and solvents.</p><p>Overall, the spray granulator market is witnessing steady growth due to the increasing demand for customized granules with specific properties and the continuous innovation in granulation technologies.</p></p>
