On the other hand, PTFE Nonwoven filters are made from polytetrafluoroethylene materials, offering superior protection against bacteria and viruses. Both types are widely used in medical-grade protective masks due to their excellent filtering capabilities and durability, making them essential components in ensuring maximum protection for users against harmful pathogens.</p></p>
