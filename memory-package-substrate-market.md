<p><p>Memory Package Substrate Market Types include WB BGA (Wire Bond Ball Grid Array), FC BGA (Flip Chip Ball Grid Array), 3D IC (Three-Dimensional Integrated Circuit), and WL CSP (Wafer Level Chip Scale Package). </p><p>WB BGA involves wire bonding and mounting chips on a grid array. FC BGA involves flip chip technology for increased performance. 3D IC integrates multiple layers of circuits vertically for improved density and performance. WL CSP allows for packaging of chips at wafer level, reducing costs and improving efficiency. These market types cater to different needs and requirements in the memory package substrate industry.</p></p>
