<p><p>Fire-proof sealants are used to prevent the spread of fire in buildings by filling gaps and cracks in various structures. The elastomeric type sealants are flexible and provide a strong seal against fire, while intumescent type sealants expand when exposed to heat to form a protective barrier. Both types of sealants are important in fire safety measures, as they help contain fire and prevent it from spreading rapidly throughout a building. Each type offers different benefits and is used in specific applications to improve fire resistance.</p></p>
