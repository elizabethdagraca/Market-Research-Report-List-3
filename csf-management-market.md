<p><strong>The CSF Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CSF Shunts</li><li>CSF Drainage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CSF management market includes two main types of products: CSF shunts and CSF drainage systems. CSF shunts are implantable devices designed to divert excess cerebrospinal fluid away from the brain to another part of the body where it can be absorbed. On the other hand, CSF drainage systems are temporary devices used to remove excess fluid from the brain or spinal cord to relieve symptoms of conditions like hydrocephalus. Both types of products play a crucial role in managing conditions related to CSF abnormalities.</p></p>
