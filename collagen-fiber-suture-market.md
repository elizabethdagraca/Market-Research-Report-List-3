<p><p>Collagen Fiber Suture market is divided into two types: Monofilament and Multifilament. Monofilament sutures are single strands of collagen fibers that are smooth and easy to handle, making them ideal for use in delicate surgeries. On the other hand, Multifilament sutures are made up of several strands woven together, providing flexibility and knot security. Surgeons can choose between these two types based on the specific requirements of the procedure, ensuring optimal outcomes for patients.</p></p>
