<p><p>AC current transducers can be categorized into two main types based on their functions: Single Function and Multi-Function. Single function transducers are designed to measure only one specific parameter, such as current amplitude. On the other hand, multi-function transducers are capable of measuring multiple parameters simultaneously, such as current amplitude, frequency, and power factor. Single function transducers are more cost-effective and simple to use, while multi-function transducers offer a more comprehensive solution for monitoring and control applications.</p></p>
