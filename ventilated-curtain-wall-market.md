<p><p>The Ventilated Curtain Wall market consists of two main types - Unitised Type and Stick Built Type. </p><p>Unitised Type involves prefabricated panels that are assembled off-site and then transported to the construction site for installation. This type offers fast and efficient installation and is suitable for high-rise buildings.</p><p>Stick Built Type, on the other hand, involves constructing the curtain wall on-site piece by piece. This type allows for more customization and flexibility in design but may take longer to install. Both types offer benefits depending on the project requirements.</p></p>
