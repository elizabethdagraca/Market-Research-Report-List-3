<p><strong>The Transmission Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reciprocating</li><li>Rotary</li><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Axial Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transmission compressor market is segmented into four main types: reciprocating, rotary, centrifugal, and axial flow compressors. Reciprocating compressors use a piston-cylinder mechanism to compress gas. Rotary compressors operate by trapping gas between rotating elements. Centrifugal compressors use radial flow to compress gas with the help of impellers. Axial flow compressors use a series of rotating and stationary blades to compress gas in a linear direction. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different applications based on efficiency and performance requirements.</p></p>
