Claus Process with Tail Gas Treatment further reduces sulfur emissions by treating the tail gas from the Claus unit. Tail Gas Treatment involves additional processes to recover sulfur from the tail gas stream. These technologies are crucial for reducing sulfur emissions and meeting environmental regulations in industries like oil refining and natural gas processing.</p></p>
